Habitat Modeling

MISK provides a variety of services in habitat modeling, employing methods like overlay analysis and buffering. Our habitat modeling services are widely used for conservation and landuse decisions in both the private and public sectors.

Overlay Analysis

We use multi-layer overlay analysis to determine the best and most likely habitat of the species in question. The information provided by these analyses help to inform decision-making and provides guidance for habitat conservation and protection.

Buffering

Applying buffers provides insight into habitat location. A buffer applied to rivers can show riparian habitat, or a buffer applied to roads can show areas which are not likely habitat. This information provides a better understanding of where habitats are in a landscape and where species may occur.
